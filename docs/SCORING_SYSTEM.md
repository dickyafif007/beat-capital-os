# Beat Capital Scoring System

# Objective

The scoring system converts multiple market signals into one simple score.

Final Score Range:

0 - 100

Higher score = Better opportunity.

---

# Score Components

## Fundamental Score

Weight:

40%

Factors:

- Revenue Growth
- Net Profit Growth
- ROE
- ROA
- EPS Growth
- DER
- PBV
- PER

---

## Technical Score

Weight:

30%

Factors:

- EMA Trend
- MACD
- RSI
- Volume
- Breakout
- VWAP
- Bollinger Band

---

## Sentiment Score

Weight:

15%

Factors:

- News
- Analyst Rating
- Sector Trend
- Economic Events

---

## Smart Money Score

Weight:

15%

Factors:

- Foreign Flow
- Broker Summary
- Accumulation
- Distribution

---
